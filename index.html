Welcome to Country Lane Kennel, where we let the country bring out the best in your pet!

We are located in Forestville, Ontario. (between Turkey Point and St. Williams) On a secluded 50 acres, at the end of a dead-end road.

My family and I live on the premises, so we are always close by to answer to your pet's needs.

I have always had a passion for animals, since I was a little girl. It all started on my family farm, bringing home baby bunnies, snakes, owls, and even black birds that I had found on my daily adventures with my German Shepard, named Tiger. Taking care of them or nursing them back to health became a high priority, with the help of my mom and dad. My mom, on a 4 hour bottle feeding schedule for the bunny, and my dad digging up the WHOLE yard for dew worms to feed the black bird. I am sure that is where my love for animals came from! Their dedication and devotion to animals, has made an ever lasting impression on me!

Now I have a farm of my own, with a loving environment for my chickens, horses, dogs, and cats.

Formerly I was a PSW for 24 years taking care of the elderly and the sick. I have learned even more about compassion, dedication, patience, and love than I thought was possible. I am now carrying on those life lessons full-time in the animal world.

I will devote myself to the care and needs of each and every pet that comes through my doors at Country Lane Kennel.

Thanks, Julie Ferris
